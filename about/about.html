If you're looking for my resume, it's here.

If you're wondering if the "design" of this website is intentional, I can assure you that it most certainly is.

I happily live a command line lifestyle and I firmly believe that this choice greatly enriches my computational life, although my commitment is more to programmability and a keyboard-driven workflow than it is to the rejection of non-textual pixels (but this site does look awesome in elinks though).

I came of age as a programmer in a REPL-driven, clojure infatuated, haze, but have since come to appreciate the value of static type systems as a valuable tool in the programmer's arsenal.

My computational life is driven by two main questions:

  • What can computers do?
  • What is the coolest thing I can do with a computer?
